Your very own Filter Bubble!

Automatically mute videos where the title fits a certain pattern. Edit the filter text below, cutting it down to a portion that would show up in other videos of the same series.

For example: Munchies: Hedonistic Hipster Chef #25 would be cut down to Munchies to filter the entire series (sorry, VICE).

You can also choose whether the filter should apply to all channels or just the one that you were selecting a video from.

If you want to change filters later on, you can do that in the Settings
